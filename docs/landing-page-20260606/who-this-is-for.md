## Who This Is For
• Late starters who always loved singing but never got the chance to learn it properly
• Self‑taught singers who want clarity, structure, and science instead of vague advice
• Bollywood/Hindi music lovers who want to sing in a modern playback style
• People who want to understand their voice, not just memorize exercises
• Creators — singers, producers, videographers — who want to collaborate and grow together
• Anyone who wants honest, practical, no‑nonsense guidance instead of “just do riyaaz for 10 years”
